Create BINGO Cards in Batch

Use the text box below to enter cards in batch

Use the following syntax:

  • Start with the name of the card
  • Then, enter a forward slash - "/"
  • Finally, enter the numbers on the card, separated by a space
    • Enter the numbers column-by-column, from top-to-bottom
    • So, start with column "B" (top-to-bottom)
    • Then, do column "I" (top-to-bottom)
    • etc...
  • NOTE: Do NOT enter a value for the Free Space

EXAMPLES:

Card #1 / 1 2 3 4 5 21 22 23 24 25 33 34 36 37 46 47 48 59 50 61 62 63 64 65

Card #30 / 1 2 3 4 5 21 22 23 24 25 33 34 36 37 46 47 48 59 50 61 62 63 64 65
